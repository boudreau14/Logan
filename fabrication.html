Lathes, Grinders, & Saws

Logan Machine is one of the best equipped machining operations in the northeastern United States. We have a broad range of tools including 20 specialized lathes. The number and variety of manual lathes we have to serve our customers provides maximum versatility. We can turn pieces up to 84 inches in diameter and up to 10 feet in length. At Logan, we think our capability is matched only by our precision. Close tolerance work is a Logan Machine Company specialty. Our people have the technical expertise and the necessary equipment to perform a wide variety of grinding operations, including surface, ID-OD, blancher, jig, and many other types. For the convenience and cost savings that come with superior capability, look no further than Logan.

In our miscellaneous tool and equipment area, we offer similar versatility. Logan is equipped to provide complete and convenient service to our customers, including a heavy-duty saw area with heavy crane capacity, keyseaters and broach sets for keyways, and a large assortment of drill presses. And they're all located in one plant, to ensure faster delivery and lower costs. These are the “extras” that make Logan Machine right for your business.

Milling Machines

Logan Machine's milling department is fully equipped with everything from small Bridgeport mills to 60-foot-long floor mills, giving us the flexibility to meet any and all of our customers' individual application requirements. A climate-controlled environment and regularly scheduled machine calibration ensure the accuracy of our mills. Even our largest machines run within 0.005" over 60 feet of horizontal travel, an unmatched level of precision. Logan's milling department also features computerized scheduling, which enables us to maintain an outstanding reputation for on-time delivery.

In order to give our customers the best products possible, Logan Machine has made a substantial investment in state-of-the-art technology and equipment in all areas of our business, including the milling department. All Logan mills have digital readouts, and all horizontal and floor models utilize built-in rotary tables for accuracy and consistency. By running a multi-shift operation, Logan Machine provides efficiency that shortens lead times. But quality is always our first priority, so we maintain an in-house, fully equipped inspection department to guarantee that Logan quality is built into every part before it is shipped to our customers.

CNC Mills & Lathes

To give Logan customers all of the advantages of the latest technology, we are constantly upgrading and expanding the availability of CNC equipment. We use Edgecam and Pro-e Cad Cam software that give us the ability to use our customers' own cad model. Our knowledgeable operators are highly qualified and experienced, and at Logan Machine, we pride ourselves on the expertise of every member of our staff. By placing the best technology in expert hands, we have been able to reduce programming time by approximately 30 percent. The end result is simple; Logan Machine supplies customers with better parts at a lower cost.

Keeping on top of the latest technology is an important part of our commitment to customers. All Logan CNC mills have either Fanuc or Mitsubishi controls, as well as built-in tool changers. Our machines also have rotary attachments and 4-axis capability. These features give them superior speed, accuracy, and capability. By constantly upgrading tooling in this and other areas of our shop, we stay ahead of our competition and serve our customers better.

We have CNC lathe capability up to 60˜ in diameter and all of our CNC lathes are equipped with sophisticated Fanuc controls and contouring capability. With skilled operators using technically advanced equipment, Logan is able to handle any machining challenge.

Laser Cutting

One of the newest machines to Logan Machine is the Mitsubishi 3015 LVPlusII-45CF-R 5X10 Laser Cutting System. This Laser lets us cut a wide range of material and thicknesses With speed and accuracy. Because of its accuracy most parts can be finished on the laser saving time and additional machining cost. Logan Machine also utilizes CAD software for nesting and complex shapes making the most out every sheet of material. With the ability To cut up to 1” thick steel plate and the auto loading 5x10 material table make this machine a nice addition to our machine list.

Welding & Sheet Metal Fabrication

Logan Machine's complete welding department can tig, mig, stick, braze, plastic weld and silver solder. We have the ability to handle weldments of many types in aluminum, hot and cold finish, or stainless steel. Logan's experienced welders are skilled and can weld 0.5" fillets in one pass, enabling us to be very cost effective. Unlike many other welding shops, at Logan we hire only qualified welders and fitters. This ensures the consistency and precision our customer require. And because we have a machining “frame of mind”, you can depend on Logan people to pay close attention to dimensions and other details, no matter how small. This enables us to produce the finest weldments in the business.

Logan's fully equipped sheet metal department is primarily used to fabricate guards and cabinets. Since most guards are built during final assembly, this department works closely with our assembly operation. Like all Logan departments, our sheet metal facility has a level of versatility that can accommodate any customer order with speed and precision, which are the true hallmarks of a Logan job.
